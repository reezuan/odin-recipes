Fried Rice

Description

This is egg fried rice. It's a simple fried rice recipe that can be made with everyday ingredients like eggs, leftover rice (it's best with leftover rice!), salt, pepper, and soy sauce. And it can be made in just 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Rice (preferably day-old or leftover), or fresh made rice can be used too.
  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green onions
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Pre-heat the wok
  3. Fry the egg liquid
  4. Fry the rice
  5. Add the seasonings
  6. Add scallions
